Cost Control
See where every riyal goes.
Then save it.
Six dedicated cost reports that turn production data into actionable savings. Know exactly what to cut, what to negotiate, and where waste is costing you money.
SAR
Waste costs you real money. Now you can see exactly how much.
Waste in SAR
Converts waste percentages into actual riyals lost per production run. You see "5% waste" but don't feel it — seeing "SAR 2,400 lost this month" hits different.
Ingredient Cost Concentration
Shows the top 5 ingredients that make up 80% of your recipe cost. Focus supplier negotiation on the few items that actually move the needle.
Substitution Opportunities
Flags expensive ingredients that appear in only 1-2 recipes. Easier to replace with cheaper alternatives without disrupting your whole portfolio.
Bulk Savings Potential
Ingredients shared across 3+ recipes with estimated monthly volume. Negotiate volume discounts with real data — not guesswork.
Batch Size vs Waste
Correlates batch sizes with waste percentages. Find the optimal batch size where waste drops — and stop producing at inefficient scales.
Cost Trend by Brand
Tracks whether each brand is getting cheaper or more expensive over recipe versions. Catches cost creep before it kills your margin.
Quality Assurance
Lab-grade QA built into every recipe
Physical, chemical, microbiological, and sensory testing — all tracked per recipe, per batch, per trial stage.
Physical & Chemical
pH, Water Activity (Aw), Brix, Viscosity — each with spec ranges, actual results, and automatic pass/fail verdicts.
Microbiology
Total Plate Count, Yeast & Mould, E. coli, Salmonella — with CFU/g specs and compliance tracking.
Sensory Stage Gates
Two NPD tasting checkpoints — Kitchen Test and Factory Trial — with pass/conditional/fail verdicts. Full sensory panels run in SensoryHub (linked directly from RecipeHub).
Shelf Life Studies
Timeline tracking at Day 0, 1M, 3M, 6M, 12M. Parameters monitored at each checkpoint with pass/fail at every stage.
Allergen Detection
Automatic allergen scanning from ingredient names. Detects nuts, dairy, gluten, soy, sesame, eggs, fish, mustard, and more. Only visible on approved recipes — QA-managed.
Nutrition Facts
Per 100g nutritional panel with energy, fats, carbs, protein, sodium, and %Daily Value. Appears on approved recipes only — after QA validation.
Certificate of Analysis
One-click COA generation. Lab report printing with all test results, verdicts, and QA sign-off. Full audit trail for regulatory compliance.
