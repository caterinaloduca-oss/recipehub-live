Production Steps8 Steps
1
🧼 Hygiene🌡️ Temperature
Equipment Preparation & Sanitation
Ensure all mixing vessels, pipelines and filling equipment are CIP-cleaned and sanitised per SAN-001 protocol before commencing production. Verify equipment calibration logs are up to date.
CIP Temperature
85°C minimum
Hold Time
20 minutes
Responsible
Sanitation Lead
2
⚖️ Weigh✅ QC Check
Raw Material Weighing & Verification
Weigh all dry ingredients on calibrated floor scales. Each ingredient must be verified against the batch ticket by two operators (4-eyes check). Record actual weights in production log.
Total Batch
400.000 kg
Scale Tolerance
±0.050 kg
Verification
4-eyes check
🛑 CCP 1 — Correct ingredient weights are a Critical Control Point. Any deviation >0.5% requires supervisor sign-off before proceeding.
3
⏱️ Time
Dry Blend — Spice & Seasoning Mix
Add all dry spices and seasoning ingredients to the ribbon blender in the order specified on the batch ticket. Blend until homogeneous. Sieve through 2mm mesh before transfer.
Blender Speed
Medium — 45 RPM
Blend Time
8 minutes
Mesh Size
2 mm
4
🌡️ Temperature⚠️ Danger
Base Liquid Preparation
Combine water and vinegar in the jacketed mixing vessel. Heat to 60°C while stirring at low speed. Add salt and dissolve completely. Verify pH before proceeding.
Target Temp
60°C
Stirrer Speed
Low — 20 RPM
Target pH
3.8 – 4.4
🛑 CCP 2 — pH must be verified in-line before adding dry blend. Log result on QA checkpoint sheet QA-F-002.
5
💡 Tip⏱️ Time
Incorporation of Dry Blend into Base
Slowly add the dry blend to the liquid base while mixing at medium speed. Ensure no clumping — if lumps observed, increase speed briefly then return to medium. Add oil phase last.
Addition Rate
Slow — over 5 min
Mixer Speed
Medium — 35 RPM
Mix Time
15 minutes total
6
✅ QC Check⚠️ Danger
In-Process QC Check
QA technician takes sample for pH, Brix and viscosity checks. Product must be within spec before proceeding to filling. Organoleptic check by QA and NPD sign-off required.
pH Target
3.8 – 4.4
Brix Target
18 – 22 °Bx
Viscosity
800–1200 cP
⚠️ If any parameter is out of spec, HOLD product and notify QA Manager and NPD immediately. Do not proceed to filling.
7
⚖️ Weigh📸 Photo Check
Filling & Packaging
Fill into designated packaging format per pack spec. Check fill weights every 15 minutes. Apply batch code and best-before date label. Confirm label compliance before pallet build.
Fill Weight
Per pack spec
Weight Check
Every 15 min
Label Check
Every pallet
8
🌡️ Temperature⚠️ Danger
Blast Freezing & Storage
Transfer filled and sealed product to blast freezer immediately after packing. Freeze to core temperature of −18°C before palletising. Store in frozen warehouse at −18°C ±2°C.
Blast Freeze
−35°C / 90 min
Core Temp Target
−18°C
Storage
−18°C ±2°C
🛑 CCP 3 — Core temperature must reach −18°C before product leaves blast freezer. Record on HACCP log HA-F-008.
